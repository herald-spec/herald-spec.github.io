Frequently ask questions

How long does EverLast take to deliver?
Your items can be delivered to your home address or to the address that you provided when placing your order on the EverLast.com website. Your items will be delivered as soon as payment for your order has been registered, within 5 working days for standard deliveries and 2 working days for express deliveries.
What is EverLast's return policy?
EverLast would like you to be pleased with your purchase and will gladly refund any unopened or gently used items within 30 days of purchase through our website. EverLast.com purchases cannot be exchanged or returned in-store.
What do the EverLast perfumes smell like?
The only EverLast perfume presented in a round bottle, Niche de EverLast is a floral fragrance featuring pink pepper, jasmine and amber patchouli. Heightened with vanilla notes, the scent is bold, fresh and flowery. Inspired by the unpredictability of chance and time, this perfume is a stunning combination of bold notes.
